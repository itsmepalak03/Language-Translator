Our Story

About LinguaAI

We believe language should never be a barrier to connection, opportunity, or understanding.

Mission

Making the world's languages accessible to everyone

LinguaAI was built with one goal in mind: remove friction from cross-language communication. Whether you're a traveler navigating a foreign country, a business expanding globally, or a student studying abroad — we provide instant, accurate, and free translation at your fingertips.

We use LibreTranslate, a fully open-source translation engine, so you can trust that your data is handled responsibly. No hidden costs, no subscriptions, no tracking.
